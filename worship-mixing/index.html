Hi, I'm David

Founder and Mix Engineer

I've been involved with creating worship music for as long as I can remember. Like many musicians, I got my start playing in church. From there, the love of music expanded into a love of music production, which I focus on today.

In college, I worked at a worship radio network in the Pacific Northwest, where I trained my ear for what works in the genre, and learned what programmers and playlist curators are looking for in a polished worship song.

Now, as a mix engineer, I want to give you the same quality that you hear the biggest worship artists have. Please reach out today about our worship mixing and mastering services and make some amazing, impactful music!
