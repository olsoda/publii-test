Graham Cochrane on How To Make Passive Income as an Artist

David Ryan Olson

I’ve got an exciting podcast episode I couldn’t wait to share with you.

We talk with the amazing Graham Cochrane of The Recording Revolution about how artists can build sustainable, passive income online.

Graham has turned The Recording Revolution into a million-dollar-a-year business while working less than 5 hours a week – and he thinks you can do the same thing as an artist. Imagine a life where you make MORE money and stress less about the financial side of your music business!

If anything is clear from the COVID crisis, it's that you shouldn't put all your eggs in one basket. Relying ONLY on shows for your income isn't the best idea. It's time to diversity!

In this episode, we break down the importance of building an online component to your music business. In fact, if you aren't building an email list and promoting your own products, you're leaving money on the table!

Many artists are easily making six figures online by building an audience and pitching products and opportunities directly to fans. This might be on Patreon or by building their own fan clubs. Want to learn more about how to do that? Listen to this episode.
