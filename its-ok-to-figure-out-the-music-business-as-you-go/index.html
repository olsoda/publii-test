It's Ok To Figure Out The Music Business As You Go

David Ryan Olson

Ever feel like you're flying by the seat of your pants when it comes to the music business?

That's ok! Nobody has it all figured out – especially in music. What matters is still pressing on, moving forward, and accepting you're going to have to experiment (and make mistakes) along the way.

This is the most healthy way to approach life (and something I personally struggle with a LOT). If you wait to have everything figured first, you're gonna be waiting a long time, my friend. Just go for it and stop focusing on perfection!

We recently had Wild Rivers on Music Business Mindset. I love that during our conversation they were extremely open-handed about how they figured out a lot of things as they went – and made some mistakes along the way!

Listen to their story and hear some of the lessons they learned along the way!
